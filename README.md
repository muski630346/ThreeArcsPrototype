 CROWNVERSE: The Three Realms Protocol

A multi-arc combat simulation game built in Unity where players choose one of three realms and fight AI-driven enemies using realm-specific abilities powered by mystical crowns.

---

  Game Overview

CrownVerse is a combat simulation game where players select one of three realms — **Modern**, **Mythological**, or **Village** — and battle adaptive AI enemies. Each arc represents a different era and combat philosophy, all connected through a unified AI system that adapts to player behavior in real time.

---

 Game Arcs
 Modern Arc
- Futuristic urban combat arena with sci-fi city environment
- Neon lighting, tech-enhanced battleground, drones and robotic enemies
- **Abilities:** Speed boost, Energy slash, Tactical shield
- **AI:** Adapts to player attack patterns with dynamic difficulty scaling

 Mythological Arc
- Ancient divine battlefield with temple ruins and floating platforms
- Sacred aura effects and fire torches
- **Abilities:** Divine strike, Lightning power, Mythic rage mode
- **AI:** Behavior trees with emotion-based combat (aggressive/defensive)

 Village Arc
- Traditional rural combat setting with open village ground
- Huts, trees, and daylight combat
- **Abilities:** Farming tool combat, Agility dodging, Earth-based stun
- **AI:** Crowd-based coordination, flanking, optimized pathfinding

---

 Crown Selection System

At the start, players choose one of three crowns:

| Crown | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Iron Crown** | Dash ability — burst speed for 0.3 seconds |
| **Neon Crown** | Speed boost — increased movement for 3 seconds |
| **Mystic Crown** | Projectile power — ranged attack ability |

Each crown modifies combat style, ability set, animation effects, VFX color theme, and AI reaction pattern.
